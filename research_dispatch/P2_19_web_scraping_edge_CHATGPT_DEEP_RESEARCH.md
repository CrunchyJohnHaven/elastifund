# P2-19: Information Sources for Prediction Edge
**Tool:** CHATGPT_DEEP_RESEARCH
**Status:** READY

## Prompt

```
For an AI prediction market trading system, research the best publicly available information sources that could provide an edge:

1. NEWS APIS: What real-time news APIs exist? (NewsAPI, GDELT, EventRegistry, etc.) Cost, coverage, latency.
2. SOCIAL MEDIA: Can X/Twitter API, Reddit API, or other social data predict market movements?
3. GOVERNMENT DATA: What government data sources (census, BLS, FRED, NOAA) are useful for prediction markets?
4. EXPERT FORECASTS: Are there aggregated expert forecast databases we can pull from?
5. SEARCH TRENDS: Can Google Trends data predict prediction market movements?
6. BETTING ODDS: How do sports betting odds from major sportsbooks relate to Polymarket prices?
7. POLLING DATA: For political markets, what's the best real-time polling data source?
8. FINANCIAL DATA: How do financial indicators (VIX, yield curve, etc.) relate to prediction market outcomes?

For each source: API access, cost, data quality, latency, and estimated prediction improvement.
```
