# P0-03: Optimal Market Selection — Which Markets Are Most Predictable?
**Tool:** CHATGPT_DEEP_RESEARCH
**Status:** READY
**Expected ARR Impact:** +15-30% (trade only the most profitable market types)

## Research Question
Our bot trades across all Yes/No binary markets on Polymarket. But some market categories are inherently more predictable by AI than others. Which categories should we focus on, and which should we avoid?

## Prompt for ChatGPT Deep Research

```
I'm building an automated prediction market trading bot on Polymarket that uses LLM probability estimation. I have backtest data showing a 64.9% win rate across 532 resolved markets.

I need deep research on:

1. MARKET CATEGORY ANALYSIS: Which categories of prediction markets are most predictable by AI models? Consider:
   - Political elections (US, international)
   - Economic indicators (Fed rates, GDP, inflation)
   - Sports outcomes
   - Technology milestones (product launches, regulatory decisions)
   - Celebrity/entertainment events
   - Weather events
   - Crypto/financial markets
   - Scientific/medical events
   - Geopolitical events

2. For each category, research:
   - What is the typical market efficiency? (How quickly do prices reflect public information?)
   - Where does AI have an informational edge over the market?
   - What is the typical resolution timeframe?
   - What is the typical liquidity on Polymarket?
   - Are there academic papers studying prediction accuracy in this domain?

3. ACADEMIC LITERATURE: Find papers on:
   - LLM forecasting accuracy by domain (Metaculus, GJOpen, Polymarket studies)
   - Superforecasting techniques applicable to automated systems
   - Market efficiency in prediction markets by category
   - Information cascades and mispricing in thin markets

4. Provide a ranked list of market categories from most to least profitable for an LLM-based bot, with supporting evidence.

5. What SPECIFIC types of questions within each category offer the highest edge? For example, "Will X bill pass the Senate?" vs "Will X candidate win the presidential election?" — which has more mispricing?

Format as a structured report with citations.
```
