# P0-25: Draft Investor Agreement & Fund Documents
**Tool:** COWORK
**Status:** READY
**Priority:** P0 (needed before accepting capital)

## Prompt

```
Draft a complete set of documents for a small prediction market trading fund accepting friends & family capital:

CONTEXT:
- AI-powered prediction market trading system on Polymarket
- Backtested win rate: 64.9%, estimated ARR: +128% to +469%
- Starting with $75 personal capital, planning to accept $10K-$100K from investors
- Solo operator/manager
- Trading begins ~March 10, 2026

DOCUMENTS NEEDED:

1. INVESTOR AGREEMENT / SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT:
   - Terms of investment (minimum investment, lock-up period)
   - Profit sharing: suggest 70/30 split (70% to investor, 30% to manager as carry)
   - Management fee: 0% or 1% annually
   - Withdrawal terms (quarterly with 30-day notice?)
   - Loss provisions (what happens if capital decreases)
   - Liability limitations
   - Dispute resolution

2. PRIVATE PLACEMENT MEMORANDUM (simplified):
   - Strategy description
   - Risk factors (comprehensive list)
   - Manager background
   - Fee structure
   - Past performance disclaimer ("simulated results")

3. OPERATING AGREEMENT:
   - If using LLC: operating agreement terms
   - Manager rights and responsibilities
   - Investor rights (reporting, withdrawal, voting)

4. QUARTERLY REPORT TEMPLATE:
   - Performance summary
   - Strategy updates
   - Risk metrics
   - Market outlook
   - Fee calculation

5. KEY TERMS TO DECIDE:
   - What profit split is standard for small quant funds?
   - What lock-up period is reasonable?
   - How to handle negative months (high-water mark?)
   - What reporting cadence (monthly, quarterly)?

Make all documents professional but understandable by a layperson investor. Include standard legal disclaimers. Note: these should be reviewed by a lawyer before use.
```
