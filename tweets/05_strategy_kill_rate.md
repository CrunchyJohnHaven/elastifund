# Tweet 05 — Strategy Rejection Rate
**Pillar:** Strategy Autopsies
**Priority:** High (honesty as brand differentiator)

---

We tested 12 strategy families for prediction market trading.

9 rejected. All of them.

Kill reasons:
- Insufficient signal count (<50 trades)
- Negative out-of-sample expectancy
- Collapse under ±20% cost stress test
- Calibration error > 0.2
- Regime decay

The backtest looked great. Post-cost, post-fee, out-of-sample reality didn't.

If your strategy hasn't survived automated kill rules, you don't have a strategy. You have a hypothesis.

**Notes:** This is the "we're different" tweet. Everyone publishes wins. Publishing a 75% rejection rate builds trust that what survives is real.
