It gives you the architecture to find your own. We document our live results honestly, but the real value is the hypothesis testing pipeline, kill-rule discipline, and strategy evaluation framework. Those are transferable to any market.

No. The methodology applies to any market with quantifiable outcomes: crypto, equities, options, sports, insurance. Prediction markets are our testing ground; the framework is general.
