# The Seven Sentences

These are the seven sentences the homepage should probably say first.

A stranger should understand the project after reading just these.

---

**1.**
We built an AI system that trades on prediction markets—platforms where people buy and sell shares on real-world events like elections, weather, and economic outcomes, with share prices reflecting crowd-estimated probabilities.

**2.**
The system estimates the true probability of events without seeing what the crowd thinks, then bets when it finds gaps between its estimate and the market price (the crowd's estimate).

**3.**
We tested this approach on 532 historical markets and the system would have been right 68.5% of the time, producing consistent profits—but this is backtested performance, not actual money made yet.

**4.**
We're currently running the system on real markets with simulated trades (fake money) to ensure the infrastructure works, and the next phase is deploying real capital to accumulate 50+ resolved trades so we can validate whether the edge is real.

**5.**
The core edge is exploiting systematic crowd mispricing: people overpay for speculative outcomes they want to happen, underpay for boring likely outcomes, and anchor to initial prices even when new information arrives.

**6.**
We've built safety guardrails (daily loss limits, position caps, circuit breakers) and invested months in getting the fundamentals right—anti-anchoring architecture, statistical calibration, Kelly criterion sizing—rather than chasing perfection.

**7.**
If the edge validates in live trading, profits are directed to veteran suicide prevention organizations—the mission is to use a real mathematical edge for real social impact, not to build a company for company's sake.

---

## Why These Seven

**Sentence 1** answers: What does the system trade on?

**Sentence 2** answers: How does it work?

**Sentence 3** answers: Does it actually work? (Honest: backtested yes, live not yet)

**Sentence 4** answers: Where are you now? (Honest: validation phase, not proven yet)

**Sentence 5** answers: What's the edge? Why might this work?

**Sentence 6** answers: What differentiates you? (Risk discipline + fundamentals, not hype)

**Sentence 7** answers: What's the goal? (Mission alignment, not pure profit)

These seven sentences are:
- **Honest** about what we've proven (backtest) vs. not proven (live)
- **Clear** about what the system does without jargon
- **Complete** enough that a stranger gets the picture
- **Credible** because they acknowledge limitations
- **Compelling** because they explain both the thesis and the mission
