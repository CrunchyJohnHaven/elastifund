A self-improving agentic operating system for real economic work.

Elastifund turns research, experiments, and execution into searchable evidence so trading and non-trading agents can improve with every run. The system ships with policy-governed autonomy, publishes its benchmarks, and runs in paper mode by default until evidence clears the next gate.

Two worker families share the platform: trading workers and non-trading workers (JJ-N). Improvement is the product, and every useful run feeds the same evidence layer.

Product definition

Two worker families. One Elastic-backed substrate.

The project is broader than a trading bot. Trading workers and non-trading workers share the same memory, evaluation, observability, workflow, and publishing layers.

Trading workers

Research, simulate, rank, and optionally execute under policy.

The current trading lane reports , uses maker-only execution, and remains blocked until first closed trades and structural evidence clear the gate.

Non-trading workers

JJ-N is the first-class front door for agentic labor.

Phase 0 focuses on a narrow revenue-operations worker with human approval, compliance controls, and honest public metrics.

Elastic substrate

System memory, evaluation, observability, and publishing.

Better agents require better data, better memory, and better evaluation. That is why Elastic is the foundation, not an afterthought.

Trust model

Policy-governed autonomy with public evidence.

The system runs in paper mode by default, keeps humans above the loop for capital transitions, and shows exactly where claims come from.

JJ-N

The non-trading worker is the first-class front door, and its shape is explicit.

JJ-N is not pitched as an unbounded entrepreneur. Phase 0 is a narrow revenue-operations worker for one high-ticket service loop, with every outbound action held behind approval and every stage measured like an actual operating system.

1

Account Intelligence

Find, enrich, and score accounts so the system learns where real commercial attention belongs.

2

Outreach

Draft compliant messages, queue them, and keep sends behind human approval while the lane is still proving itself.

3

Interaction

Classify replies, book time, and hand humans a context-rich brief instead of an empty calendar invite.

4

Proposal

Turn discovery into scoped offers without pretending pricing, commitments, or legal boundaries can be automated away.

5

Learning

Compare predicted value to actual outcomes and only promote workflow changes when the evidence is real.
