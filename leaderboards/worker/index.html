Metric definitions

The public scoreboard is defined before the lane goes live.

These are the metrics JJ-N will publish once the worker is instrumented. Each needs a clear definition, source of truth, and confidence band.

Accounts researched

Target accounts enriched and scored.

Counts only when account evidence is written to the registry.

Qualified leads

Prospects that pass the scoring framework.

Public count must map to explicit fit and compliance thresholds.

Messages sent

Approved outreach actually delivered.

Reply rate belongs beside it, not hidden in a later dashboard.

Meetings booked

Calendar holds accepted by a real prospect.

Show rate must accompany raw meeting counts.

Proposals sent

Scoped offers generated from approved templates.

Drafts do not count until they are reviewed and sent.

Revenue won

Verified cash from accepted work.

No pipeline inflation, no blending signed intent with cash.

Gross margin

Revenue minus delivery cost.

A worker can close work and still be a bad business if margin disappears.

Time to first dollar

Feedback density is part of the product.

Faster learning cycles matter as much as topline volume in Phase 0.

Five-engine model

JJ-N is designed as a revenue-operations worker, not an unbounded entrepreneur.

1

Account Intelligence

Find, enrich, and score target accounts so the system learns which buyers deserve attention.

2

Outreach

Draft and queue compliant messages. In Phase 0, all sends stay behind human approval.

3

Interaction

Classify replies, schedule meetings, and prepare context-rich briefs for human operators.

4

Proposal

Turn discovery into scoped offers without letting pricing or legal commitments go unsupervised.

5

Learning

Compare predicted value to actual outcomes and update the playbook only when the evidence is real.

Approval system

Humans stay above the loop for commercial outreach.

Phase 0 requires approval for every outbound action. That is the right boundary for sender reputation, compliance, and honest governance.

Compliance

CAN-SPAM, suppression, sender identity, and rate limits are product features.

The public worker board should only light up when the compliance layer and CRM schema are strong enough to support it.

Worker route

Define the scoreboard first. Earn the revenue story later.
